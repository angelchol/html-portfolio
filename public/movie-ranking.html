Best Movies According to Angela

My top three movies of all time

The Lord of The Rings

These movies with its story explores the good and evil. Teach us about frindship, sacrifice and hope.

This is how I rank them:

1. The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King (2003)
2. The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring (2001)
3. The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers (2002)

Hobbit

I really love its blend of adventure and fantasy storytelling.

This is how I rank them:

4. The Hobbit: An Unexpected Journey (2012)
5. The Hobbit: The Desolation of Smaug (2013)
6. The Hobbit: The Battle of the Five Armies (2014)

Harry Potter

My childhood favorite that I still watch to this day.

My top three of them all

1. Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (2004)
2. Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2 (2011)
3. Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (2005)
